ABOUT ME

I am a student at the Stephen M. Ross School of Business at the University of Michigan, studying global marketing and strategy. In a constantly changing world, I have discovered the importance of culture in business, and I have devoted my academic career to incorporating culture in a business sense. I best feel that I can help the world through the marketing and business strategy lessons I have learned at school, and can't wait to put that to practical use. I created this website as a way to briefly display some interesting projects I have worked on, and highlight a few experiences that have made my academic journey unique. During my time at Michigan, I have been fortunate enough to hold a couple of research positions, and have opportunities to study internationally in Morocco and Nicaragua. I wanted to build this website as a way to put my newly learned coding skills to functional use, and I have found this is a perfect intersection of my interests.

International Trade Theory

During February 2017, I spent 12 days in Morocco doing strategy fieldwork, and attending academic lectures surrounding the growing importance of international trade in Africa. Morocco has a unique location, situated between the top of Africa just under Spain. The government has decided to take advantage of this location, and is starting to act as a gateway country for trade between the two continents. My experience was incredible, and I learned more about life, culture, and friendship than ever before. Download my final project here.

The Catastrophe Theory

Last summer, I did a 3 month marketing science research project under Marketing Professor Dr. John Branch. In my research, I was tasked with finding parallels between contemporary marketing strategy, and a mathematical phenomenon known as the Catastrophe Theory. The Catastrophe Theory explains "the breaking point" or a shift on the mathematical plane, but Dr. Branch wanted me to connect the two and look for factors to cause the consumer's "breaking point" when it comes to marketing and advertising.
